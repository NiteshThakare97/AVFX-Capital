Cryptocurrency

What cryptocurrencies can you
trade with us?

Cryptocurrency or crypto, is digital money that uses secure technology for
transactions and operates on a decentralised system, using a ledger to record transactions.

Bitcoin (BTC)

Bitcoin (BTC) was the pioneer cryptocurrency, introduced in 2009 by the presumed pseudonymous creator Satoshi Nakamoto.

Ethereum (ETH)

Introduced in 2015, Ethereum is a blockchain platform featuring its own cryptocurrency, Ether, and serves as a foundation for decentralized applications.

Litecoin

Faster than Bitcoin, Litecoin is recognized for speedy payments and greater transaction capability.

Ripple

Created in 2012, Ripple is a distributed ledger system with uses extending beyond cryptocurrency, collaborating with banks for diverse transactions.
