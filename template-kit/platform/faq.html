To open a new order in MetaTrader 4, you can:

Press F9 on Windows or fn+F9 on MacOS.
Right-click a symbol in the Market Watch window and choose "New Order."
Click the "New Order" button in the toolbar.

In the New Order window, select your symbol, set the volume in lots, and specify your Stop Loss and Take Profit levels. Choose the order type: for market execution, click "Sell by Market" or "Buy by Market." For a pending order, select the type (Buy Limit, Sell Limit, Buy Stop, or Sell Stop), set the trigger price, and press "Place."

To enable one-click trading, go to Tools -> Options -> Trade tab, check "One-Click Trading," accept the terms, and press OK. You can then right-click a chart, enable One Click Trading, and place market orders directly from the chart.

For pending orders from the chart, right-click at the desired price level, choose the pending order type, and set the parameters.

In the Trading app, select the symbol, set the volume, and press Sell or Buy. For pending orders, tap "Pending Order," choose the type, symbol, volume, and price, and set Stop Loss, Take Profit, and Expiry limits. Press "Place Order."

MetaTrader 4 supports market orders (Buy and Sell) and pending orders, which include Buy Limit, Sell Limit, Buy Stop, and Sell Stop.

To modify a position, double-click the S/L or T/P fields in the Trade tab, or right-click the position and select "Modify or Delete." Enter your desired Stop Loss and Take Profit levels and press "Modify."

In the Trading app, go to the orders tab, select the order to modify, tap the pencil icon, and adjust the Stop Loss and Take Profit settings. Press "Modify Order."

Remember:

  • For a sell order, Stop Loss must be above the current ask price, and Take Profit below.
  • For a buy order, Stop Loss must be below the current bid price, and Take Profit above.

If the levels are too close to the current price, you won't be able to modify the position. Check minimum distances by right-clicking the instrument in Market Watch, selecting Symbols, and viewing Properties.

You can also modify orders directly on the chart by enabling "Show Trade Levels" .

In the Trade tab, right-click the order and select "Close Order," then confirm in the pop-up window. In the Trading app, go to the orders tab, select the order, and press the cross icon. Enter the volume and press "Close."

MT5 supports two main order types: market and pending.

  • Market orders: Execute immediately at the current price.
  • Pending orders: Execute once the price reaches a predefined level.

Six types of pending orders:

  • Buy Limit: Buy at a price lower than the current.
  • Buy Stop: Buy at a price higher than the current.
  • Buy Stop Limit: A combination of Buy Stop and Buy Limit.
  • Sell Limit: Sell at a price higher than the current.
  • Sell Stop: Sell at a price lower than the current.
  • Sell Stop Limit: A combination of Sell Stop and Sell Limit.
